About Celtec

Our Story

Founded with a passion for technology and a commitment to customer satisfaction, Celtec has become the trusted electronics repair partner for students, businesses, and professionals across Kenya. Based at Moi University Main Campus in Eldoret, we combine expert technical knowledge with genuine care for every device that comes through our doors.

With years of experience and a team of qualified technicians, we've repaired thousands of devices and built lasting relationships with our customers. We believe in transparent pricing, quality workmanship, and making technology accessible to everyone.

Why Choose Celtec?

  • Fast Repairs - Get your device back quickly without compromising quality
  • Affordable Pricing - Competitive rates designed for students and businesses
  • Experienced Technicians - Skilled professionals with years of expertise
  • Quality Parts - We use genuine and high-quality replacement components
  • Trusted Service - Your device is in safe hands with our certified team
  • Wide Service Range - From phones to laptops, we handle it all
